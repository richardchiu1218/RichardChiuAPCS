UFC Fighter Lookup

The UFC is the premiere mixed martial arts, promotion company.

This website gives the most up to date information about the top 3 fighters and the Champion of any weight class in the UFC.

The following information is true as of April 20, 2024, a week following the 300th UFC event.

There are 11 Weight Classes: 8 Male and 3 Female.

When selecting rank, 1 represents the Champion, 2 represents the #1 ranked, 3 represents the #2 ranked, and 4 represents the #3 ranked.

The fighter assesements are recognition of areas of their skill and accomplishments.

Being a "Striker" means that the fighter is primarily known for their explosive fighting style or base. Some fighting bases/styles, for example, would be Boxing, Kickboxing, and Muay Thai.

Being a "Grappler" means that the fighter is primarily known for their isometric fighting style or base. Some fighting bases/styles, for example, would be Wrestling, Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, and Sambo

Being "Freestyle" means that the fighter is too well rounded to be considered either a "Striker" or "Grappler".

The information provided is based on interviews, podcasts, and official information put forth by the UFC.
